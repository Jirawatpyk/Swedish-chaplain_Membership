# Accessibility Requirements Quality Checklist: F3 — Member & Contact Management + Smart Features

**Purpose**: "Unit tests" for the **quality of accessibility requirements** in F3's spec, plan, contracts, and UX artefacts. Validates whether WCAG 2.1 AA conformance requirements (POUR: Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, Robust) are **complete, clear, consistent, measurable, and traceable** — NOT whether the implementation passes axe-core.
**Created**: 2026-04-15
**Feature**: [spec.md](../spec.md)
**Depth**: Comprehensive — compliance audit (WCAG 2.1 AA; EU Web Accessibility Directive implications for Swedish users)
**Audience**: Maintainer + `/speckit.review` + `/speckit.staff-review` agents at Merge Gate
