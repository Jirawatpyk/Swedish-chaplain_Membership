## Executive Summary

Phase 4 ships PromptPay end-to-end on top of card. Two prior fix-rounds closed 4 Critical + 8 Important findings (financial integrity, Result<T,E> contract, PCI log hygiene, cross-method orphan handling). PCI SAQ-A scope is preserved. Constitution Principle I/III/IV/VI/VIII/IX/X all pass. **However, the `reliability-guardian` audit identified 1 HIGH blocker that prior reviews missed**: when `cancelPaymentIntent` returns a `retryable` error (network timeout, Stripe rate-limit), the use-case still falls through to `updateStatus(canceled)`, creating a **DB-canceled / Stripe-pending drift** that the existing stale-pending sweep cron is not designed to handle (the cron expects Stripe-canceled / DB-pending — opposite direction).
