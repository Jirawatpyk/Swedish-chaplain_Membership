## Executive Summary

**Verdict**: ❌ **CHANGES REQUIRED**

Eight independent specialist agents + a manual correctness pass surfaced **9 blockers** (3 documentation, 3 UX/a11y, 1 architecture, 1 security, 1 data integrity), **13 warnings**, and ~30 suggestions across all 10 review passes. The core Phase 3+4 implementation is fundamentally sound — Constitution Principle I (NON-NEGOTIABLE tenant isolation) passes all 5 sub-clauses, strict-tx ACID + dual-write fallback works end-to-end, Clean Architecture is enforced, and the round-5 staff-review fixes (R001–R012) hold cleanly. The blockers cluster around: (a) F6 has no parity entries in `docs/observability.md` + `docs/runbooks/cron-jobs.md` (F7/F8 precedent requires both before flag-flip), (b) two WCAG 1.4.3 contrast failures on status badges that ship to every admin event-detail page, (c) one heading-level skip (h1 → h3) on the detail page, and (d) one CHECK constraint gap + LIKE-wildcard escape gap that future bugs could exploit.

None of the 9 blockers requires a re-architecture; all are surgical fixes (≤30 lines code change each, plus ~200 lines of docs). Estimated total remediation: 1–2 working sessions before `/speckit.ship`.
