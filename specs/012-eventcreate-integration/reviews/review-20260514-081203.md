## Findings — 🟡 Warnings (1)

### WARN-01 — Skeleton stepper alignment diverges from real Stepper (sub-CLS, no a11y impact)

| Severity | File:line | WCAG |
|---|---|---|
| 🟡 WARN | `src/app/(staff)/admin/settings/integrations/eventcreate/loading.tsx:39` | n/a (CLS sub-threshold; no WCAG criterion violated) |

**Issue**: Round 9 S-10 closure claimed the skeleton `<ol>` matches the real `<Stepper>` layout. UX agent spot-checked and found a 1-line divergence:
- `loading.tsx:39` uses `className="flex items-center"`
- Real `<Stepper>` horizontal layout: `"flex-row items-start gap-0"` (`src/components/ui/stepper.tsx:65`)

`items-center` vs `items-start` causes the connector strip to vertically centre in the `<li>` row, while the real stepper has `items-start` + `mt-2` label offset. Visual swap on slow networks is a small vertical jump. **CLS impact < 0.01 at typical content density** — below docs/ux-standards.md § 2.1 CLS-0 hard threshold but worth aligning.

**Recommended fix** (~3 LoC):
```tsx
// loading.tsx:39
<ol
  className="flex flex-row items-start gap-0"
  aria-label={t('loading')}
>
  {[1, 2, 3].map((n) => (
    <li key={n} className="flex min-w-0 flex-1 flex-col items-center text-center">
      {/* circle + label per real stepper */}
    </li>
  ))}
</ol>
```

**Status**: Non-blocking. Fix in a follow-up round or roll into Phase 6's first commit.
