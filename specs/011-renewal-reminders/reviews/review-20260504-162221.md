## Executive Summary

Round 5 remediation correctly closed the prior round's findings but **introduced 3 new Blockers** that must be fixed before flag-flip:

1. **B-R5-1 (Correctness)** — `PAN_LIKE_RE = /(?:\d[\s-]?){13,19}/` counts **repetitions of "digit + optional separator"**, NOT digits. Thai bank reference format `YYYYMMDD-NNNNN` (e.g. `KTB-20260504-12345`) gets falsely blocked because 13+ digit/separator units match. **Production blocker — would refuse legitimate Thai bank references.**
2. **B-R5-2 (Tests)** — Contract test `tests/contract/renewals/admin-mark-paid-offline-route.test.ts:192` still asserts `body.error.reason === 'plan_not_found'` but Round 5 W-02 scrubbed `reason` from the HTTP body. Test contradicts the change it verifies → either currently RED (and the test runner is silently skipping) OR latent regression on next run.
3. **B-R5-3 (Spec sync)** — `data-model.md:626` was NOT updated to include `new_expires_at` in `RenewalCycleCompletedOfflinePayload` even though Round 5 S-09 synced `audit-port.md`. Two contracts now disagree.

Plus 1 high-severity finding from security: `f4Reason` field is logged server-side but is NOT in `REDACT_PATHS` in `src/lib/logger.ts` (only `reason` and `*.reason` are covered) — F4 internal schema/column fragments leak to Sentry/Grafana.
