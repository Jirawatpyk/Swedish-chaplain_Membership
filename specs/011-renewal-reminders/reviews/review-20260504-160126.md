## Executive Summary

Phase 3 is functionally complete with strong defensive engineering (231/231 unit tests, p95=293ms vs 500ms budget, Constitution v1.4.0 all 10 principles PASS, 6 Complexity Tracking entries documented). The 4 review rounds successfully closed 50+ findings. However, this fifth-pass staff review surfaces **2 new blockers** in the original Phase 3 implementation that prior rounds did not catch:

1. **B1 (Spec)** — AS3 reason badges `grace_expired` / `payment_failed` missing from `CLOSED_REASONS` enum; LapsedTab silently falls back to `'lapsed'` for these cases.
2. **B2 (Correctness)** — `findById` inside `cancel-cycle.ts` opens a NEW nested `runInTenant` transaction, so the post-lock re-read does NOT participate in the lock-holding tx — defeating TOCTOU protection.

Plus **3 important security warnings** (W-01..W-03) — payment_reference + F4-error reason + cancel-reason CRLF injection — that should be hardened before flag-flip.
