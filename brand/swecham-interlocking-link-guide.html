The Interlocking Link

Two woven rings — Chamber Navy and Crown Gold — reading at once as a Thailand–Sweden partnership and as the twin “C”s of Chamber of Commerce.

What it means

  • Partnership. Two equal rings, woven — neither dominates. A bilateral chamber, two nations linked.
  • Chamber of Commerce. The twin rings echo the two “C”s of the name.
  • Navy + Gold. Sweden's national colours, also reading as corporate trust + value.
  • One continuous form. No detail to lose — survives down to a 16px favicon.
