Brand Identity

SweCham / TSCC — Enterprise Logo System

A bilateral chamber sits between two identities — Thailand and Sweden — and serves businesses that expect institutional credibility. The system pairs a calm, corporate navy with Sweden's gold, a restrained Thai red accent, and a clean geometric wordmark. Four expressions share one DNA so the brand scales from a 16px favicon to a conference backdrop.

The four concepts

Recommended · Primary mark

Interlocking Link

Two woven rings — navy + gold — read at once as a partnership between two nations and as the twin “C”s of Chamber of Commerce. Abstract, modern, owns a distinctive silhouette. This is the lead symbol.

Heritage · Formal

Tre Kronor Emblem

Sweden's three crowns over a Thai-red baseline, in a chamber-style roundel. For seals, certificates, membership cards and formal correspondence where gravitas matters.

App icon · Avatar

SC Monogram Tile

A rounded navy tile with the SweCham monogram and gold rule. Built for the product UI, browser tab, social avatar and mobile home-screen icon.

Reversed · Dark

Reversed Mark

White-and-gold variant of the primary mark for dark UI, navy backgrounds and photography. Every asset ships in positive and reversed forms.

On dark

Navy is the surface as often as it is the ink. On navy, the wordmark reverses to white and the navy ring becomes white while the gold holds — preserving the woven read.

Typography

SweCham
THAI–SWEDISH CHAMBER OF COMMERCE

Wordmark: geometric humanist sans, 700 weight, −1.5 tracking. Tagline: 600 weight, +2 tracking, all caps. Production stack uses Inter / Segoe UI; the platform UI already ships this family, so the brand and the product stay visually identical.

Scales cleanly

Usage

DoKeep clear space ≥ the ring height around the lockup · use navy or white backgrounds · reverse to white+gold on dark · use the SC tile below 64px where the wordmark would break.
Don'tRecolour the rings · stretch or rotate the mark · place the positive lockup on busy photos · fill areas with Thai red · add drop shadows or gradients to the wordmark.
